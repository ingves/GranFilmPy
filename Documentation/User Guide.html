Introduction to GranFilmÂ¶

GranFilm is a Fortran90 software developed by Ingve Simonsen and Remi Lazzari. It calculates the optical properties of a substrate which support a 2D lattice of nanoparticles.

GranFilm allows you to play on:

  • the geometry of the particles (radius,truncated / coated / spheroidal particles)
  • the structure of the lattice
  • the materials used for the ambient medium, the substrate and the particles

Figure 1: Kind of system modeled by GranFilm

GranFilm mainly returns:

  • the reflection, transmission and absorption coefficients
  • the scalar potential
  • the polarizability of one particle of the lattice
